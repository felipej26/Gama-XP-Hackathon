Gama Academy

Le Wagon

Le Wagon is a coding school founded in 2013 and run by Boris, Romain and Sébastien in Paris, France. Le Wagon’s mission is to bring technical skills to creative people. Our 9-week coding bootcamp gives you the ability to build a prototype of any idea you might have. No technical background needed, just your motivation.
